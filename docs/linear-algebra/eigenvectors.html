Eigenvectors

Definition

An eigenvector is a nonzero vector $\vec{v}$ such that there exists a number $\lambda$ so that $A \vec{v} = \lambda \vec{v}$. The $\lambda$ is known as an eigenvalue, and together they form an eigenpair.
