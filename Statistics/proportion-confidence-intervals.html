Proportion Confidence Intervals

General Idea

As $n$ becomes large, the sampling distribution of $\hat{p}$ becomes normal: $$N\left(p, \frac{p(1-p)}{n}\right)$$ We also know that, for a normal distribution, the z-score is a pivotal quantity that follows a normal distribution. Therefore, to find a two-sided confidence interval, we can use $$P\left(X\right)$$
